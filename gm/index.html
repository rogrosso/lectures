Geometric Modeling

This section handles different aspects of modeling three-dimensional curves and surfaces. Typical application areas are computer-aided design (CAD), reconstruction of surfaces from sensor data (reverse engineering), and construction of smooth interpolants. The lecture covers the following topics:

  • Polynomial curves
  • Bézier curves
  • B-splines curves
  • Rational Bézier and B-Spline curves
  • Tensor product surfaces
  • Subdivision Surfaces

The implemented algorithms are given below, including code snippets. We strongly recommend interacting with the graphics to understand the algorithm and its purpose.
